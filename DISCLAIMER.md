This project demonstrates security scanning and compliance assessment techniques
against cloud infrastructure owned by the author. The fictional company `"NovaPay`"
is a scenario construct for portfolio demonstration purposes.

All AWS and Azure resources scanned by this tool exist in subscriptions and
accounts owned by the author. No third-party infrastructure was assessed.

Framework mappings (GDPR, UAE PDPL, Essential Eight) are based on publicly
available regulatory text and security guidance. Mappings reflect the author's
interpretation and should not be considered legal advice or audit certification.
